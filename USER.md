## AI Skills Studio

### The Vision
An all-in-one platform where entrepreneurs, creators, and small businesses can **launch a business with AI** and master the skills—no prior AI experience needed.

### Target Audience
- Entrepreneurs
- Creators  
- Small businesses who are AI noobs

### The 2-Layer System

**Layer 1: Education**
- Practical AI skills taught through instructor-led bootcamps
- Partner with popular entrepreneurs & influencers as instructors
- Users browse courses, tutorials, and instructors
- Office hours: One-on-one paid sessions with instructors

**Layer 2: Creation Tools**
- Built-in image & video creation tools
- **Secret Sauce:** Prompt engine that makes ANY input output high-end, cinematic results—no prompt engineering skills needed
- All latest/hottest tools included with membership

### Business Model
- **Pricing:** $1.00 for 7-day trial → Pro subscription
- **Brand Ambassador Program:** Ambassadors earn $10/mo per pro user referred (network marketing effect)
- **Community:** Discord mastermind for ambassadors + live events/conferences for networking

### Brand Philosophy
**"Entrepreneurship first, tech second"**
- Teach people how to be entrepreneurs
- Tools are included but secondary to the mindset/skill building
- Focus on community, hustlers, people who want to change their lives
