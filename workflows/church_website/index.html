ABOUT OUR CHURCH

A Place to Belong

Grace Community Church has been serving our community for over 50 years. We believe in creating a welcoming environment where everyone can experience the love of God, grow in their faith, and find their purpose.

Led by Pastor Michael Thompson, our church is committed to biblical teaching, authentic worship, and serving our local community through various outreach programs.
