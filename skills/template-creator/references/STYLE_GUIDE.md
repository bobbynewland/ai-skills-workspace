# Design Style Guide by Business Niche

Quick reference for creating on-brand templates across different industries.

## Fashion (Kids Clothing)

**Colors:**
- Primary: Pastel pink (#FFB6C1), baby blue (#87CEEB), mint green (#98FB98)
- Accent: Bright coral (#FF6B6B), sunshine yellow (#FFE66D)
- Background: Soft white (#FFFAF0), light cream (#FFF8DC)

**Typography:**
- Headlines: Playful, rounded fonts (Poppins, Nunito)
- Body: Clean sans-serif (Open Sans, Roboto)
- Sizes: Headlines 48-72px, body 24-32px

**Visual Elements:**
- Polka dots, stars, playful patterns
- Rounded corners, soft shadows
- Cartoon mascots or illustrations
- Photo frames with decorative borders

**Messaging Tone:**
- Enthusiastic, parent-friendly
- "Adorable deals for your little ones"

## Food & Restaurant

**Colors:**
- Primary: Warm reds (#DC143C), oranges (#FF8C00), yellows (#FFD700)
- Accent: Fresh greens (#228B22), browns (#8B4513)
- Background: Cream (#FFFDD0), wood textures, chalkboard black

**Typography:**
- Headlines: Bold, appetizing (Oswald, Bebas Neue)
- Body: Readable serif or sans (Lora, Montserrat)
- Sizes: Headlines 56-80px, prices prominent

**Visual Elements:**
- Food photography focus
- Rustic textures, craft paper
- Utensils, ingredients as accents
- Steam/smoke effects

- Mouth-watering descriptions
