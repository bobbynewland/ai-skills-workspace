## SECTION 1: 2026 DESIGN TRENDS

### 1.1 Typography - THE LOUDEST ELEMENT

**Trend:** Oversized, attention-grabbing typography that IS the hero image.

**Styles Dominating:**
- **Ultra-Bold Sans-Serif:** Heavy weights, condensed widths (impact, impact condensed)
- **Chiseled Serif:** Modern twist on classic - sharp, authoritative
- **Retro Groovy:** 70s-inspired but modernized (popular in fashion/beauty)
- **Handwritten Script:** Personal, authentic feel (Fenty, Fashion Nova use this)

**Sizing Rules:**
- Headlines: 80-200% of image height
- Negative space is luxury - let type breathe
- Overlap type with imagery for depth

**Color Pairings:**
- White text on saturated backgrounds
- Neon accents (lime green, hot pink) on black
- Metallic (gold, silver) on dark jewel tones

### 1.2 Texture - THE TACTILE REVOLUTION

**Trend:** Moving beyond flat colors to rich, tactile surfaces.

**Texture Categories:**

| Texture Type | Vibe | Use Case |
|--------------|------|----------|
| Marble/Limestone | Luxury, editorial | Fashion, beauty |
| Concrete/Urban | Raw, streetwear | Street brands, sneakers |
| Gradient Mesh | Modern, fluid | Tech, automotive |
| Film Grain | Nostalgic, authentic | Heritage brands |
| Liquid/Glass | Futuristic | Automotive, tech |
| Animal Print (subtle) | Bold, sexy | Fashion Nova, Fenty style |
| Cracked/Earth | Organic, raw | Natural products, food |

**Application Techniques:**
- Gradient overlays (20-40% opacity)
- Noise/grain filters
- Multi-layer blending modes
- Masking for depth

### 1.4 Composition Layouts

**Layouts That Convert:**

**1. THE HIT (Most Popular)**
- Full-bleed hero image
- Typography as overlay (bottom or center)
- Simple CTA below
- Used by: Fashion Nova, Revolve

**2. THE SPLASH**
- Image on one side (60%)
- Bold typography on other (40%)
- Color blocking
- Used by: Gucci, high-fashion

**3. THE GRID**
- 2-4 images in grid
- Unified by color/filter
- Carousel-ready
- Used by: Sephora, Ulta

**4. THE STATEMENT**
- Centered subject
- Type above and below
- Heavy negative space
- Used by: Luxury brands
